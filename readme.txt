Includes an in your face "add your comment" link at the bottom of each of your posts (not pages)

== Description ==

So you want to get more comments on your blog posts, but most visitors have trouble finding how to get from the front page of your blog to the comments area of an individual post.

This plugin solves that by including a dedicated and in your face link at the bottom of each of your posts (not pages) that points the visitor directly to the comment response area.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `AddCommentLink` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
